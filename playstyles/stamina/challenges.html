Stamina players are gluttons for punishment so they have devised several painful challenges to get their rocks off.

The Dirk Challenge

The player must pass JackOff Marathon expert [20] from Cranked Pastry as their first song after not playing ITG for at least six months. This grew to be a popular joke among some, a few of which attempted the challenge for themselves. Interestingly, Dirk himself has never passed The Dirk Challenge officially, although the official conditions of the challenge aren't universally agreed upon.

Backstory

A player known as Dirk took a long hiatus from playing ITG. After a couple sessions and warming up, he passed JackOff Marathon expert [20] from Cranked Pastry. He later joked that he, "just stepped onto the pad after six months [of no ITG] and passed JOM."[1]

The Dirk Challenge Passes

Zetorux passed The Dirk Challenge with an 82.20% on October 26, 2019 on a DDR X2 cabinet after multiple attempts, each over six months after the previous attempt.[1][2]The pads were unmodded, and the panels were noticeably recessed.[3]

Other Attempts

JDongs failed with a 52.61% on April 18, 2019.[1]

The Havoc Impact Challenge

The player must pass Havoc Impact expert [16] from Beast Aoreo Beast 2 as soon as you wake up in morning after a full night's sleep.

The Habanero Challenge

The player must pass Moonearth expert [17] from Disbalance 3 while eating an entire, raw habanero pepper.

On February 18, 2012, Zetorux made an attempt but failed with a 33.69% when he ran off the pad in agony.[4]

The 17x17 Challenge

The player must pass 17 stamina 17s in a single set. Because footspeed 17s can be completed so quickly, only stamina 17s should be selected (roughly 4 minutes or longer songs only).

The Salary Man Challenge

The player must play stamina content continuously from 9 AM to 5 PM, "putting in your eight hours".

The Encoder Challenge

The player must attempt to do as many push-ups as possible during the 32 measure break in Encoder from the Pendulum pack series.

Beremy made a video compilation of recorded attempts in 2020.

 

The MAX 300 Birthday Challenge

On their birthday, the player must play MAX 300, from DDRMAX Dance Dance Revolution 6thMix, once for each year they have been alive.
