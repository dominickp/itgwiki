General Communities

  • The DDR + ITG Players Facebook Group is a very large Facebook group that focuses on DDR and ITG as a whole.
  • The Dance Game Youth is a Discord community for ITG, DDR, and PIU players aged 21 or under. Yearly ITG remote tournaments are held in this server, and is a smaller community of <40 people. If you are interested in joining, contact Azirixx#6186 on Discord.

Playstyle-focused Communities

  • Stamina Nation (SN) is a Discord community focused on ITG stamina and footspeed. It's run by Archi (the godfather of stamina) and is the home base for East Coast Stamina events (including Stamina RPG).
  • International Timing Collective (ITC) is a Discord based community focused on ITG tech and fantastic attack (FA). It's the home base for the International Timing League events (formerly ECFA).
  • The NotITG Mods clubbe is a Discord community focused on ITG mods. It's the home base for NotITG and UKSRT events.

Dance Pad DIY and Modding Communities

  • /r/DIY_Dance_Pads is a small community centered around building DIY dance pads.
  • This Facebook group if for anyone who needs advice or wants to talk about repair, mods, and DIY pads. For all Dance Dance Revolution, StepMania, ITG, PIU arcade or home pads.

Marketplace Communities

  • The Official Buy/Sell/Trade group for rhythm game players is a more broad group for listing rhythm game products for sale. It is one of the best resources to buy used dance game pads within the community.
  • BEMANI Buy/Sell/Trade is a Facebook group focused on selling rhythm game items.

Arcade Machine Maintenance Communities

  • Rhythm Game Cabs is a Discord community focused on discussion of rhythm game arcade cabinets and their upkeep. It is a useful resource if you have an arcade machine and have maintenance questions, or for those looking to buy a machine/parts.
  • The Private Dance Games Arcade Owner (ITG, DDR, PIU, TechnoMotion) Facebook group is invite-only (but rather large) and focuses on troubleshooting and technical help for private arcade owners.

Software-focused Communities

  • The GrooveStats Discord is a place where people can discuss GrooveStats or get technical help with the gslauncher integration.
  • There are several places to discuss Project OutFox: such as /r/OutFox or Project OutFox Rhythm Engine Discord.
  • /r/Stepmania is a subreddit for StepMania discussion
  • /r/DanceDanceRevolution is a subreddit for DDR discussion but ITG/StepMania comes up from time to time

Regional Communities

  • DDR + ITG Players (Ontario) is a Discord community for 4-panel players in Ontario. They also have a Facebook group.
  • Polish Dance Game Community (PDGC) is a Discord community for players based in Poland.
  • Lisia Nora is a Polish community focused on events, conventions and newbie-to-pro player conversions.
