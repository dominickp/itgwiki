Helblinde is a stream pack by Zaia.

This was my first artist-specific pack since Powerglove in 2010. This pack came with a companion book that can be downloaded here.

This pack was followed by two sequels: Helblinde 2016 (2016) and Helblinde PDTA (2019)

 

 

Structure

Helblinde is structured around 200bpm songs of about 4 minutes in length. All simfiles but one have four difficulties. Most expert level charts are in the 15-17 range.

The chart description fields contain the stream breakdowns.
