City Pop is an upcoming stream pack by Zaia. It is a genre-specific pack focusing on 1980s Japanese American-influenced pop music, known by the loose genre “city pop”, as well as early 1990s proto-Eurobeat/Eurodisco/Italo disco music.

The expected release date is Q1-Q2 2025 (as at November 2024).

 

 

Structure

Each simfile in the pack contains 4 charts, including all mixes. Lowers are produced using the “cut-stream lower” style.

Development

The City Pop project began on June 22, 2019, when Shiawase no Monosashi (VANTAGE- Edit) was stepped. It has continued very slowly, with new charts being used in ECS events in ones and twos. A large portion of work was done in November 2023, as well as in May 2024.

It's my largest project, so it is taking a long time to produce. Please await the delivery with excitement!

Artistic Influences

The charts are written with fairly moderate patterning - most fall within the middle band of the slidey-steppy spectrum, especially given their tempos enable them to be able to support more pattern diversity. There are outliers, but most maintain access to the full range of stream patterns available.

Graphics

The visual theme is based around 1980s Japanese pop art, inspired particularly by artists like Hiroshi Nagai.
