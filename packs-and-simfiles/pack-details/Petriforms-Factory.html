Reception and Retrospect

The release date was intentionally chosen. It was in the middle of Stamina RPG 3, which meant that the attention of most players would be occupied with the event. I wanted to test the ability to release a pack in the middle of a high-engagement event like Stamina RPG and see how new pack drops could perform despite that handicap.

As expected, it did not achieve good optics, even with the Helblinde pedigree attached to it. I don't believe the pack ever did pick up like its predecessors had, but the mixes gained a comparatively decent amount of playtime. Most of the exposure this pack received was through its use in ECS events.

I have a positive impression of Helblinde PDTA, although in retrospect I feel I could have filled out the songlist without needing to resort to music from other artists.
