Cirque du Huayra is a pack by Zaia. It is a “rolling release” pack, which means that it receives new content on an ongoing basis.

It is specifically a pack of restepped charts only (simfiles first made by other people that I later stepped and released myself).
It is named after the Pagani Huayra, and is themed after the Japanese manga Made in Abyss.

 

 

 

Structure

This pack differs from the others in that it has no fixed structure other than that every simfile has at least one chart, and that all charts are classified as stream charts. Some simfiles may have lowers, and difficulty levels span a broad range.

The main commonality of this pack is that every simfile in it had previously been stepped before; not necessarily just by someone else, but I could have restepped my own older charts and placed them here too.

Development

Cirque du Huayra started when Stamina Showcase 2 was announced.
