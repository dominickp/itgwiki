Helblinde 2016 is a stream pack by Zaia.

It is the second pack in the Helblinde series. Preceded by Helblinde (2014) and followed by Helblinde PDTA (2019).

 

 

Structure

Helblinde 2016 is structured identically to Helblinde. Most simfiles have four charts, and all lowers are the “cut stream lower” type. 

Two songs, Aura and Heaven's Fall, received resteps. These are subtitled “2016 Rebuild”.

Chart description fields contain the stream breakdown.

Graphics

The same concept used for the first pack was employed here. I took the text logo from The True Prophet and overlaid it against a wallpaper of an abstract skyscape. The girl in this one has no identity. I just looked for an image that resembled the first one. The same editing effects and composition are used and the result is a background that looks similar and adheres to the visual theme of the series, but isn't completely “the same”

Songlist

Helblinde 2016 contains 19 songs.

Reception and Retrospect

Helblinde 2016 was received as a welcome addition to the Helblinde series. Songs that were omitted in the first pack got some attention here, and personal favorites from the first pack got an updated chart. Hero was noticeably popular and I seemed to see more score posting of that one than the others.
