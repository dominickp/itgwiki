Helblinde 2016 is a stream pack by Zaia.

It is the second pack in the Helblinde series. Preceded by Helblinde (2014) and followed by Helblinde PDTA (2019).

 

 

Structure

Helblinde 2016 is structured identically to Helblinde. Most simfiles have four charts, and all lowers are the “cut stream lower” type. 

Two songs, Aura and Heaven's Fall, received resteps. These are subtitled “2016 Rebuild”.

Chart description fields contain the stream breakdown.
