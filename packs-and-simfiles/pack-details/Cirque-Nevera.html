Cirque Nevera is a generalized stream pack by Zaia.

It is the sequel to Cirque du Zeppelin (2014). It is named after the Rimac Nevera, and themed after the Japanese manga/anime series Houseki no Kuni (Land of the Lustrous).

It is the first new addition to the Cirque series in the 2020s, and the first since Cirque du Miura (2019).

It is also the the first pack in the series to feature ITG doubles charts, as well as Pump It Up singles charts and Pump It Up doubles charts.
