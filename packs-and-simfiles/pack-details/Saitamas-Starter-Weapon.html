Saitama's Starter Weapon is a stream pack by Zaia. 

It is a companion pack to Saitama's Ultimate Weapon. It is a half-speed version of the pack with lower level charts

 

 

Structure

There are two charts to each simfile, including the mixes. Lowers are “cut-stream” versions of their Expert counterparts. All individual songs were cut to between 2:00 and 2:30 in length.

Development

By October 11, 2021, Saitama's Ultimate Weapon was nearing completion when I decided that I couldn't risk that happening, so I started another pack and gave myself more work.
