Betwixt & Between is a stream pack by Zaia.

This pack was dedicated to the life of Betwixt & Between, real name 木村馨 (Kaoru Kimura), born 29 April 1983, passed away 17 September 2012.

 

 

Structure

Betwixt & Between is identical in structure to Helblinde and Helblinde 2016. Each song has four charts and the lowers are in the “cut stream lower” style.

The chart description fields contain the stream breakdowns.

Artistic Influences

The writing style of this pack is primarily inspired by its contemporaries - Dragonforce, Pendulum and Sharpnelstreamz v2 era Sharpnel (not Rebuild). 

Graphics

In Q3 2014, I started a trend with my background graphics in that they would be inspired by things outside of dance games that I liked. Rebuild of Sharpnel (2018) would be the first pack with a graphic that was inspired by something not related to dance games, using the Evangelion motifs and visual style. In Q3 2015, I had made a number of graphics for various projects, including Dragonforce Kaioken (2019) (at the time named Dragonforce SSJ2) and The Apocalypse Sampler (2015), and their graphical themes were based on Dragon Ball Z and 2001: A Space Odyssey, respectively. Contininuing the 2001 trend, I decided to use the film's visuals as a reference a second time for this project.
