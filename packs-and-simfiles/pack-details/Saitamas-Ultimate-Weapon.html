Saitama's Ultimate Weapon is a collaboration stream pack by Zaia and YourVinished. It is an artist-specific pack for the artist Saitama Saisyu Heiki (S.S.H.), who was a fan arranger of existing songs and specialized in the synth rock/metal genre.
